Pasta and cheese

The recipe for the best pasta and cheese in the world

  • pasta
  • salt
  • cheese
  • butter
  1. Put 100 grams of pasta in the boiling water for 20minutes
  2. Prepare a large plate and put a little bit of butter on it
  3. After 20minutes put the pasta on the plate
  4. Put some salt and cheese on the pasta and your meal is ready
